Thruk is Free & Open Source

Because we believe in Open Source Software, Thruk comes with:

  • GPL License
  • Free source access
  • No open core concept
  • No hidden features
  • Community driven project

If you want to be part of the Thruk community you have plenty of options.
Either by sharing your experiences on the Mailing List or in the Forum or
helping each other.
Experienced users are also encouraged to help developing Thruk
with either small pull requests on GitHub or by creating your
own plugins.
