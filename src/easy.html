Thruk is easy to use

In order to get maximum user experience:

  • We offer ready-to-use packages
  • Fully integrated into OMD (omdistro.org)
  • Flexible and modular design
  • 5 themes included
  • Easy to extend with own plugins

Thruk is developed by people who's daily job is to work with large monitoring setups. So the focus is clearly on usability and performance in large installations with thousands of hosts and services.
